Evan Iacobucci

Bio

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of City and Regional Planning at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. My work focuses on transportation behavior, specifically how people perceive and interact with the world around them, and how their attitudes and perceptions relate to their travel patterns. I am also working on projects pertaining to urban freight as well as the impacts of COVID-19 on the future of travel.
